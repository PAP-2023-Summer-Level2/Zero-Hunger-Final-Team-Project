Where Zero Hunger becomes relevant:

The countries with the most hunger are the Central African Republic, Madagascar, Yemen, and DR Congo. Even with countries around the globe being impacted by hunger, this issue is relevant when its impact can be seen in parts of every city.

When Zero Hunger becomes relevant:

The goal is needed now, there are millions of people around the world that go to sleep hungry, people who are dying of malnutrition. The longer this issue goes unsolved, the more lives are at risk and the more people who are unable to live up to their full potential simply because of the lack of resources that they have.

How Zero Hunger becomes relevant:

For each country the reason for how they got on the list of World Hunger is different but the underlying answer is: corrupt government and/or climate change.
